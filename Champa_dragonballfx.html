Breve Resumen

Champa is the God of Destruction of Universe 6. Hes accompanied by his attendant and martial arts teacher, Vados. Hes both the twin brother and constant rival of the God of Destruction of Universe 7, Beerus . Champas the main antagonist of the Universe 6 Saga, but he becomes a supporting character in later sagas.
