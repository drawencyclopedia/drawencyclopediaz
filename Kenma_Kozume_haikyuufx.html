Breve Resumen

Kenma Kozume (Japanese: 孤爪 研磨 was previously a 2nd-year student from Nekoma High. He was the volleyball teams setter and was referred to as the heart and brain of the team by his teammates. As of 2018, he is a university student and seems to have become somewhat wealthy through a varied career as a stock trader, professional gamer, Youtuber, and the CEO of his own company, Bouncing Ball Corp.
