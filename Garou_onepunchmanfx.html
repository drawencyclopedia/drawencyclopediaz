Breve Resumen

Garou is a villain, a martial arts prodigy, the self-proclaimed Hero Hunter, and a major adversary of the Hero Association. He is a former disciple of Bang but was expelled from his dojo for going on a rampage. Because of his fascination with monsters, he is commonly called the Human Monster.
