Breve Resumen

Maria Ishida is a supporting character in the Koe no Katachi series. Maria is the daughter of Pedro and Shōyas older sister. She is the niece of Shōya, and granddaughter of Miyako. In the final chapter of the manga, it is revealed that her mom is pregnant, so Maria will soon be an older sister.
