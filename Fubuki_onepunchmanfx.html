Breve Resumen

Fubuki , also known by her hero alias Blizzard of Hell is the B-Class Rank 1 professional hero of the Hero Association. She is an esper, the younger sister of Tatsumaki, and the leader of the Blizzard Group, a B-Class hero faction. She and her sister are known as the Psychic Sisters. Fubuki is one of the few people who are aware of Saitamas true strength and is currently trying to recruit him into her group. Shes also a self-declared member of the Saitama Group.
