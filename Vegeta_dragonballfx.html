Vegeta , more specifically Vegeta IV , recognized as Prince Vegeta is the prince of the fallen Saiyan race and one of the major characters of the Dragon Ball series. Regal, egotistical, and full of pride, Vegeta was once a ruthless, cold-blooded warrior and outright killer, but later abandons his role in the Frieza Force, instead opting to remain and live on Earth.
