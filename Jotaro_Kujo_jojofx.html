Breve Resumen

Jotaro Kujo is the protagonist of Part 3 and also appears in Parts 4-6. Jotaro is the third and most recurring JoJo of the JoJos Bizarre Adventure series. Jotaro is a delinquent who lives an ordinary life until the Joestar Familys old enemy, DIO, returns. Jotaro travels to Egypt in order to save his mother and stop the Vampire once and for all. Wielding the incredibly powerful Star Platinum, Jotaro is the first JoJo introduced with a Stand, and is among the most well-known characters of the series.
