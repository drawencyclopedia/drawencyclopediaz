Breve Resumen

Anna is a supporting character of The Promised Neverland. She is a fellow orphan who resided in the Grace Field House under the parental care of Isabella. Two years younger than Emma, Norman and Ray, she is a thoughtful girl who is brought into the escape plan alongside Nat, Thoma and Lannion after the truth of the orphanage is revealed. After her success of escaping Grace Field alongside 14 other orphans, Anna now seeks to find William Minerva, whom she and her family believe can grant them the liberty to live a safe and peaceful life away from the demons.
