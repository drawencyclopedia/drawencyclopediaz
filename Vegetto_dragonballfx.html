Breve Resumen

Vegito , called Vegerot in the Viz English manga, is the result of the fusion between Goku and Vegeta by the use of the Potara Earrings. Vegito is the most powerful character in the original Dragon Ball manga. His Fusion Dance counterpart is Gogeta.
